MUSEUM OF CANDY

Is there anything better than eating handfuls of candy until you teeter on the edge of sheer bliss and belly-aching agony? We say no. There's only one way to make this time-honored pastime better, and that's indulging in homemade candy. No weird preservatives, no crazy chemicals—just pure, unadulterated sugar, the way nature intended. Get ready for your high: These candy recipes will keep you buzzing.
